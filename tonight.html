Phase 1 — Week 1

Strength A: Push + Core

Monday • Friskis&Svettis Sundbyberg or Solna

Coach Marcus

First session. Go light — the last rep should feel like "I could do 2-3 more." Learn the movements. Feel where the muscles work. The weight goes up next week. Tonight you just show up and move. That's the win, brother.

Warm Up — 5 min
Warm Up Routine
  • 3 min bike or cross-trainer (light pace)
  • 10 bodyweight squats
  • 10 arm circles each direction
  • 10 hip circles each direction
  • 1 easy set of goblet squats at half weight
Main Workout
Exercise 1 of 7
Goblet Squat DONE
3 sets 12 reps 60s rest
Tap to see how to do it
Quads Glutes Core
Free weights area — grab a dumbbell from the rack (start 10-14 kg)
Hold dumbbell at your chest with both hands. Squat down until thighs are parallel to floor. Keep chest up, knees tracking over toes. Push through heels to stand.

Exercise 2 of 7
Dumbbell Bench Press DONE
3 sets 10 reps 60s rest
Tap to see how to do it
Chest Shoulders Triceps
Free weights area — flat bench + dumbbells (start 10-12 kg each hand)
Lie on flat bench, feet on floor. Hold dumbbells above chest, arms extended. Lower slowly (2 seconds down) until elbows are at 90 degrees. Press back up (1 second). Keep your back flat, don't arch.

Exercise 3 of 7
Dumbbell Shoulder Press DONE
3 sets 10 reps 60s rest
Tap to see how to do it
Similar movement — seated, pressing dumbbells overhead
Shoulders (Deltoids) Triceps
Adjustable bench set to 90 degrees + dumbbells (start 8-10 kg each hand)
Sit on bench with back support at 90 degrees. Hold dumbbells at shoulder height, palms facing forward. Press straight up until arms are fully extended. Lower slowly back to shoulders. Don't arch your back.

Exercise 4 of 7
Cable Tricep Pushdown DONE
3 sets 12 reps 45s rest
Tap to see how to do it
Triceps
Cable station — attach rope or straight bar to the high cable pulley
Stand facing the cable machine. Grip the rope/bar with both hands. Keep elbows pinned to your sides — they don't move. Push down until arms are straight. Squeeze at the bottom for 1 second. Slowly let it back up. Only your forearms move.

Exercise 5 of 7
Plank DONE
3 sets 30-45s hold 30s rest
Tap to see how to do it
Abs / Core Shoulders Glutes
Functional area — grab a mat from the rack
Get on the floor on your forearms and toes. Body forms a straight line from head to heels — like a plank of wood. Squeeze your abs and glutes tight. Don't let your hips sag or pike up. Look at the floor. Breathe normally. Hold.

Grip Strength Protocol
Exercise 6 of 7 — Grip Builder
Dead Hang DONE
3 sets 20-30s hang 30s rest
Tap to see how to do it
Forearms / Grip Shoulders Lats
Pull-up bar in free weights area or cable machine frame
Grab the bar with both hands, shoulder-width apart. Let your body hang. Relax your shoulders — don't shrug. Squeeze your hands as hard as you can. Breathe. Hold as long as possible. This directly targets your grip weakness (40 kg — target 47+).

Exercise 7 of 7 — Grip Builder
Farmer's Walk DONE
3 sets 30m walk 45s rest
Tap to see how to do it
Forearms / Grip Traps Core Shoulders
Grab heavy dumbbells (start 16 kg each hand), walk the gym floor slowly
Pick up the heaviest dumbbells you can hold. Stand tall — chest up, shoulders back. Walk slowly in a straight line for about 30 meters. Crush the handles as hard as you can. Don't rush. Sundbyberg's open layout is great for this.

Cool Down — 5 min
Cool Down Stretches
  • 2 min easy walk on treadmill
  • Quad stretch — 30s each leg (pull foot to butt)
  • Hamstring stretch — 30s each leg (foot on bench, lean forward)
  • Chest stretch — arm on wall, turn away, 30s each side
  • Dead hang — 20s (bonus grip + spine decompression)
