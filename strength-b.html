Machine park — leg press station
Sit in the leg press, feet shoulder-width on the platform. Lower the weight until knees are at ~90 degrees. Push through your heels back to start. Don't lock your knees at the top. Start light — add plates next week.

Lats (Back) Biceps Shoulders
Cable machine area — lat pulldown station with wide bar
Sit down, thighs under the pad. Grab the wide bar with overhand grip, wider than shoulder-width. Pull the bar down to your upper chest — lead with your elbows. Squeeze your shoulder blades together at the bottom. Slowly let it back up. Don't lean back too far.

Hamstrings Glutes Lower Back
Free weights area — dumbbells + stand in front of mirror for form check
Stand holding dumbbells in front of your thighs. Slight bend in knees — keep them there. Hinge at your hips, pushing your butt back. Lower the dumbbells along your legs until you feel a stretch in your hamstrings (roughly mid-shin). Squeeze your glutes to come back up. Keep your back flat the entire time — watch in the mirror.

Mid Back Lats Biceps
Cable station — low pulley with V-bar or straight bar attachment
Sit on the seat, feet on the platform, knees slightly bent. Grab the handle. Pull it to your belly button — lead with your elbows. Squeeze your shoulder blades together. Slowly release forward. Keep your torso upright — don't rock back and forth.

Hamstrings
Machine park — lying or seated leg curl machine
Lie face down (or sit, depending on machine). Pad rests behind your ankles. Curl your legs up by bending at the knees. Squeeze at the top. Lower slowly (2 seconds down) — the slow negative builds more muscle. Don't jerk the weight.
Exercise 6 of 8

3 sets 12 reps 45s rest
Tap to see how to do it
Biceps Forearms
Free weights area — light dumbbells (start 8-10 kg each)
Stand with dumbbells at your sides, palms facing forward. Curl the weights up by bending at the elbow only — upper arms don't move. Squeeze at the top. Lower slowly. No swinging your body. Stand in front of the mirror to check form.
Grip Strength Protocol
Exercise 7 of 8 — Grip Builder
Grip Crush DONE
3 sets 15 reps 30s rest
Tap to see how to do it
Forearms Grip
Free weights area — use a kettlebell (Sundbyberg has them) or weight plate
Option A: Pinch grip a weight plate — hold it between your thumb and fingers, squeeze for 3 seconds, release. Repeat 15 times each hand. Option B: Grip a kettlebell by its ball (not the handle) and hold for 10 seconds. Both brutally target grip. Your NEKO grip was 40 kg — this fixes that.
Exercise 8 of 8
Standing Calf Raises DONE
3 sets 15 reps 30s rest
Tap to see how to do it
Calves
Machine park (standing calf raise machine) or any step/platform edge
Stand on a step with your heels hanging off the edge. Rise up on your toes as high as you can — squeeze at the top for 1 second. Lower slowly until you feel a deep stretch in your calves. Full range of motion is key. Hold the wall or machine for balance.
